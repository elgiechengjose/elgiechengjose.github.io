Air Force

My name is Elgie Jose. I am a Staff Sergeant in the Air Force. I am a Dental Assistant, stationed at Osan Air Base, Republic of Korea. I am the Non-commissioned Officer in Charge of Dental Logistics, where I order supplies and equipment that is needed for the clinic and patient care. I have been in the Air Force for almost 9 years. Most of my years in the Air Force have been overseas. Before going to Korea, I was stationed at Kadena Air Base, Okinawa, Japan for 6 and a half years. At Kadena, I worked in the Pediatric section cleaning children's teeth and assisting with any further treatment, such as cavities. After 3 years of Pediatrics, I moved to Oral Surgery. We did simple treatment to complicated procedures, like wisdom teeth extractions or full facial reconstruction. 1 year later, I got moved to the Prophylaxis section where we did annual exams and cleanings for our Active Duty personnel and dependents. Another year passes, I get switched into Dental Logistics where I finished out the rest of tour at Kadena. Prior to that, I was stationed at Ellsworth Air Force Base, South Dakota for 1 year. I assisted a General Dentist with all kinds of procedures, from cavities to root canals. Joining the Air Force has been one of the best decisions I have made in my life, and I don't regret it at all. Every lesson, good or bad, I have learned from the military has made me the person I am today.
