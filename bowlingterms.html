Commonly Used Terms

When bowling, you may hear some words in the bowling alley you may not understand. Below are some commonly used words or terms you will hear in the bowling alley.

Frame
Your chance to knock down all ten pins (each frame has two attempts, except for the 10th frame)

Head Pin
The 1 pin or pin in front

Open Frame
A frame with no spare or strike

Perfect Game
12 straight strikes, resulting in a 300 game (highest possible score)

Spare
When you knock down the remaining pins on your 2nd attempt

Split
A gap between multiple pins

Strike
When you knock down all ten pins on the first throw in a frame

Turkey
Three strikes in a row
