Softball

One of the main hobbies I enjoy doing is playing softball. Out here in Korea, we have a huge softball community. We play tournaments every other weekend, consisting of at least 10 teams per tournament. It's a fun game we all enjoy playing, and we have been playing since we were kids. It has given me many opportunities to travel around Asia with tournaments in other countries. I have played in Korea, Japan, Thailand, and Philippines. My favorite one, so far, has been Philippines because I got to see family while I was out there. A few of the bat brands that we have been using are Monsta and Anarchy. There are different websites you can go through in ordering bats, but I usually go through All-American Athletics to check out exclusive bats created.
